Gameplay

Maze Adventures!

Use your finger to guide Peter through the maze avoiding walruses, dead ends and lava. A swipe or drag anywhere on the screen will move Peter. If you have the misfortune to run into a walrus or fall into lava, you lose a life and are sent back to the beginning of the maze for that level. You have five lives before you are forced to start the game again.

You will know that you have reached the end of the maze when you pass the blue star. Along the way, try to collect as many gold coins as possible. Each gold coin gives you more points than you earn just walking through the maze and ten gold coins equals an extra life.
